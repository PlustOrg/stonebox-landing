Frequently asked questions

What are credits and how do they work?

Credits are the currency of Stonebox, allowing you to use the right compute for any job. They are consumed based on the runtime you choose and how long your code runs. Here’s how they break down:

CPU Runtime: 1 credit per minute.
GPU Runtime: 50 credits per minute.

Real-world example: Your 5,000 credits on the Hobby plan can run a CPU job for over 83 hours, or a GPU job for 100 minutes. Mix and match as you need!

What happens if I go over my credit limit?

We offer simple, pay-as-you-go billing for any usage beyond your plan's included credits. You can set a monthly spending limit to prevent unexpected charges. We'll always notify you before you are billed for any overages.
